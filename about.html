About KrishiSetu

Bridging farmers to technology for sustainable growth

Empowering Farmers Through Technology

KrishiSetu is a unified platform designed to empower farmers by combining agricultural practices with cutting-edge technology. Our goal is to make crop selling simpler, boost profitability, and give access to timely insights and services.

Through real-time weather updates, AI-powered price predictions, and direct connections to buyers, KrishiSetu reduces middlemen and promotes sustainable farming.
